# Offer optimization, making the BCZ Strategies offer convert harder

Research-backed upgrade to the "1 Artifact = 1 hour with Zaal" offer. 2026-06-29.

## What the research says (verified)
- **Tiers win:** projects with 5 to 7 reward levels fund best. Three-option anchoring means ~70 to 80% pick the
  MIDDLE, and a premium top tier makes the entry tier look like a steal.
- **Sell outcomes, not hours:** value-based offers anchor to the result (a plan, a shipped thing), not time.
  "Don't charge for 20 hours, charge for what they walk away with."
- **Scarcity lifts conversions ~15%** (FOMO) and a busy calendar reads as proof of demand. BUT do NOT show a
  tier as "sold out", that demotivates later backers. Frame scarcity as "limited hours this season."
- **Premium tiers attract insiders** (private sessions, build sprints, VIP access).

## The upgraded offer (buy-threshold reward tiers on the same $10 Artifact)
Keep the $10 = 1 hour hook as the entry. Add two higher milestones to anchor and pull bigger buys. On Artizen
these are "rewards for collectors" at buy thresholds, which fits how the platform works.

- **1 Artifact, $10, One Hour.** A full hour with me on anything you want, online or in person. Bring a launch, a
  brand, a token, an event, an AI agent, or a pitch. You leave with a clear next step. (the irresistible hook)
- **5 Artifacts, $50, Deep Dive + Plan.** Everything above, and we go deep on one thing. You walk away with a
  written action plan you keep. (the middle, where most land, outcome-anchored)
- **10 Artifacts, $100, Build Sprint.** A half day building it together, online or in person, plus a month of
  async access for follow-ups. We ship something real. (premium anchor)

Light scarcity line (honest): "I can only take on so many hours this season, so the sooner you grab one, the
sooner we build." Demand-as-proof, no sold-out shown.

## Why this is better than the flat offer
- The premium tier makes $10 feel like a steal (anchoring), and the middle tier captures bigger buys.
- Outcomes (a plan, a shipped thing) make the value concrete, not just "time."
- More Artifacts per backer = more sales = more match unlocked across every fund.
- It still leads with the same simple, generous hook, so it does not lose the magic.
