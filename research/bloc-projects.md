# ZAO bloc + ZAO Fund projects - grounded research

Researched 2026-06-26 (4 parallel agents) to ground the "ZAO bloc" project list with verified facts instead of
thin notes. **Honesty rules applied (the Levitt lesson): every claim is confidence-tagged, gaps are stated, and
nothing is presented as fact unless verified.** Do not send outreach or publish a claim marked [LOW] without
Zaal confirming it first.

## Big caveat on method

Artizen project pages (artizen.fund/index/p/<slug>) are **Bubble.io client-rendered - NOT web-indexed and not
bot-fetchable.** So per-project sales/status come from ZAOOS internal **doc 843** (ZAO Fund Artizen roster
snapshot, 2026-06-11), and creator identities come from web/X search. Where a creator has no web presence, the
profile stays [LOW] - that is a real gap, not laziness.
