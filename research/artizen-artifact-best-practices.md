# Artizen Artifact best practices + ZAO Festivals analysis

Research + analysis for the ZAO Festivals Artifact. The Artifact is the $10 open-edition collectible and the one
visual a buyer sees. 2026-06-29.

## The rules + what wins (verified)
- Square 1:1. NO text, NO graphic overlays, NO logos (unless text is integral to the artwork itself).
- Can be a photo, sketch, concept art, animation, looping GIF, or video (square, MP4/MOV, under 45s).
- **Motion wins:** Artizen states "visually stunning Artifacts that use GIFs or videos perform the best." A
  looping GIF or short video outperforms a still.
- It should capture the big idea / the vibe / the ethos, and stand as a work of art or document a milestone.
- It is NOT a trailer, ad, or poster. Candid and artful beats polished marketing.
- 100% of each $10 sale goes to the creator; most sales within a fund wins that fund's prize.

## The two images on hand (analysis)
1. **Drone shot, ZAO-CHELLA, Wynwood at sunset.** Strong still: artful, golden hour, the muraled venue glowing,
   real sense of place, no text, documents the Art Basel milestone. Stands alone as a work of art. Best STILL
   option. Weakness: it reads atmospheric (city at dusk) more than "live music," and a still leaves the motion
   advantage on the table.
2. **Cipher in the Airbnb.** Candid, documentary, the crew making music together. This is the build-in-public /
   community-owned DNA in one frame. Artizen prefers documentary over marketing, so it fits. Weakness: busy and
   cluttered for a single primary Artifact. Better as a Moments social post or a second-season Artifact.

## What else we could do (ranked by impact)
1. **A short square VIDEO (the strongest move).** If the drone shot came from drone FOOTAGE, the moving aerial
   reveal of the venue at sunset would be a stunning 10 to 20 second square loop. Motion + sense of place +
   artful. This beats any still.
2. **A looping GIF from festival footage** - crowd, lights, an artist mid-set. Motion, simple, on brand (navy +
   gold, silhouettes). The "Crowd Light Surge" recipe in kit/artifact-briefs.md.
3. **A cinemagraph** - the drone still with ONE moving element (the sky drifting, lights flickering). Elegant,
   low effort, gets the motion bump while keeping the photo's composition.
4. **The drone still (cropped square)** - the fast, strong v1. Already prepared. Publish now, upgrade later.
5. **The cipher as a short clip** - the crew making the cipher, 10 to 20s, square. A documentary Artifact that
   screams community-owned.

## Recommendation
- **If a moving clip exists (drone footage or any festival B-roll): use it.** Square, under 45s, no text. Motion
  is the single biggest performance lever Artizen names.
- **If not, publish the drone still now** (it is genuinely strong) and swap to a GIF or video when there is time.
  A live project beats a perfect Artifact sitting in drafts, and you can upgrade the Artifact any time.
- Keep the cipher for a Moments post and a future season Artifact.

## Note
You must create a NEW Artifact each season, so this is a recurring asset. Build a small library of square,
no-text festival clips and stills so each season has a fresh one ready.
