# Synergies - ZAOstock / ZAO Festivals x Artizen x the ZAO ecosystem

Where ZAO's existing assets and Artizen's mechanics combine for more than the sum. Grounded in the
zaostock repo audit (ZAODEVZ/zaostock, 2026-06-23) + the Artizen playbook (doc 887). Each synergy =
the combination + the concrete move.

## 1. 501(c)(3) x Artizen match x sponsor tracks = the strongest sponsor pitch in the ecosystem

ZAOstock has **501(c)(3) fiscal sponsorship via Fractured Atlas / New Media Commons**. Stack that with
Artizen and a sponsor dollar does THREE things at once:
- **Tax-deductible** (via the 501c3),
- **Unlocks Artizen match** (every $1 sponsorship unlocks match at the week's multiplier),
- **Earns the Presenting Sponsor slot** (king-of-the-hill on the fund hero).

ZAOstock's existing **3 sponsor tracks (Main Stage / Broadcast / Year-Round)** map straight onto Artizen
fund sponsorships. **Move:** re-pitch the sponsor deck (zaostock.com/sponsor/deck) with "your gift is
tax-deductible AND doubled by match AND puts your brand on the fund hero." That is a better offer than a
plain sponsorship anywhere else.

## 2. ZAOstock artists x Artifacts = the festival is an Artifact-minting engine

ZAOstock already commits to **a recording, a photo, and a recap reel per artist**. Those media assets ARE
Artifacts (square, video/gif, no text). **Move:** every booked artist mints a $10 Artifact from their set
media; fans buy it, which unlocks match and pays the artist. The festival's media pipeline becomes its
fundraising pipeline - no extra production, the assets already exist.

## 3. Festival day = a live Fund Drive rally (Acadia traffic + livestream)

ZAOstock is free, on the sidewalk in the town **every Acadia visitor drives through (~4M in 2025)**, with a
**1K+ livestream** target and a 4,000-resident local-awareness goal. Artizen drives run Thursday-Thursday.
**Move:** time the festival (or its run-up) inside a Fund Drive; put **QR codes on-site + a livestream CTA**
-> buy a $10 Artifact -> unlock match in real time. A free festival with a giant captive audience becomes a
live boost+buy rally - exactly the crowd Artizen rewards.

## 4. WaveWarZ x Artizen = battle voting is a boost funnel

WaveWarZ (the ZAO music-battle platform) is a crowd-rally by design - fans already mobilize to vote.
**Move:** map battle energy to Artizen: battle artists mint Artifacts, fan votes translate into Artifact
buys + boosts, winners get curated into the ZAO Fund. The competition mechanic and the funding mechanic
reinforce each other (Boost Score = (sales + match) x boosts).

## 5. The ZAO bloc cross-backing = everyone climbs together

The 10 member-run projects (/community page) + ZAOstock artists form a bloc. Boosts are weekly + capped,
so **Move:** a standing weekly ritual - the crew spends its boost recharge across the bloc, one horse
gets the buy-push each drive. Reciprocal boosting lifts every member's Boost Score at once.

## 6. Aligned comp: ZAO Festivals fee structure x Artizen Fund Director cut

ZAO Festivals already pays **5% finders / 10% management / 15% combined** on sponsor dollars; Artizen pays
the **Fund Director 20% of sponsorships**. **Move:** a teammate who brings + runs an Artizen fund sponsor
can be paid from the stack - the incentives already line up, no new structure needed. Removes Zaal as the
sponsor bottleneck (the stated goal of the finders-fee doc).

## 7. Local Ellsworth relationships x Artizen sponsors + buyers

The Soul Benders play (May 24) and Heart of Ellsworth (28 events, 50+ sponsors in 2025) are a warm local
network. **Move:** local partners become Artizen fund sponsors (tax-deductible + matched); local attendees
become Artifact buyers via on-site QR. The "lead with music, no jargon" framing already works on this crowd -
keep crypto in the background, lead with "back the festival, your $10 is doubled."

## 8. S7 fund seeding x a ZAO Festivals Fund (the bigger optional play)

René is seeding new S7 funds **$10K-$50K** for deep, underserved passions. **Move (optional):** a "ZAO
Festivals Fund" for artist-led real-world music events could be that fund - seeded by René, sponsored by the
local + brand network, funding ZAOstock's artists directly. This is the upgrade path from "ZAOstock as a
project" to "ZAO Festivals as a fund." Decide later; the project ships now.

## The compounding loop

Festival media -> Artifacts -> fans buy + boost -> match unlocks (tax-deductible sponsor dollars + endowment)
-> artists paid -> better festival -> more media. Each ZAOstock cycle feeds the next, and the ZAO Fund's
match deployed becomes the proof for René (doc 886 + 887). The festival and the fund are the same flywheel.
