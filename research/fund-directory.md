# Artizen Fund Directory (full) + ZAO Stacking Map

The complete Artizen community-fund landscape - **79 funds** rendered live from
artizen.fund/index/matchfunds on 2026-06-13 (Frontier Fund Drive #3, ends Jun 18). Standings are
PER-DRIVE and brutally volatile - re-render before quoting. This replaces the earlier ~17-fund snapshot.

## Three findings that change the strategy

1. **Music/festival funds are now a crowded category - the "white space" claim is wrong.** There is a
   **We're Loud Fund for DIY Music Festivals**, plus Global Music Fund, Music + Healing Fund, Resonate
   Fund for Sonic Pioneers, SoundEscape (female sound pioneers), Confession Sessions (underground music),
   Rising Voices of Cascadia (musicians). **Correction to doc 850 / the /festivals page:** the ZAO
   Festivals Fund is NOT the only music-festival fund. Re-position on a different edge (ZAO's own
   ecosystem of events + artist-ownership + festival-as-prototype), or reconsider creating a fund at all
   vs simply curating ZAOstock/Zaoville INTO We're Loud + Greenpill + Global Music.
2. **The ZAO Fund is dormant this drive** - currently **#23, $188 raised** (29 projects, but nobody is
   collecting their artifacts this drive). The earlier "#2-#4" was a different drive. The fund only ranks
   when its projects sell. This is the real, urgent problem: activate the projects, not add funds.
3. **ZAO already has ecosystem allies running funds** - Bonfires (our knowledge-graph partner), Edge City
   (our grantee Telamon's org), DeSci Asia, Pressman Film (the 36 Cinema broker). Warm cross-curation paths.

## Implications

1. **Activate the ZAO fund's projects this drive** (it is dormant at $188) - the #1 problem, before anything else.
2. **Re-decide the ZAO Festivals Fund** - given We're Loud + Global Music + Greenpill already cover music
   events, curating INTO them may beat creating a 80th fund. Revisit doc 850's premise. (See research-audit.md.)
3. **Cross-curate aggressively** - ZAO projects now have 3-5 credible funds each. Stacking is the lever.
4. **Hit the ally funds** - Bonfires, Edge City, DeSci Asia for warm cross-backing.
