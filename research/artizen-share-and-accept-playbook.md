## 1. THE 48-HOUR LAUNCH PLAN

Launch timing is critical. Artizen's algorithm rewards early velocity and consistent momentum. Coordinate all steps for 10:00 AM ET on launch day.

### Pre-Launch (48 Hours Before)
1. Segment your 188-member community into three tiers by engagement level
   - Super Core (10-15 most active members): Send personal Farcaster/Telegram DM 24 hours before launch, referencing something specific they've done or said. Frame as "help signal momentum, no pressure." Target commitment: $100-300 in first hour.
   - Core (30-40 regular members): Telegram announcement 2 hours before launch with match mechanics and direct link.
   - Warm (remaining 100+): Public Telegram/Discord post 1 hour before with simple call to action.

2. Finalize artifact description copy
   - Lead with impact, not features: "Fund ZAOstock 2026: Independent Music Has a Home" (headline)
   - Include specific value prop: "Your $10 buy becomes ~$30 in match funding after all Artizen funds apply."
   - Detail concrete use (stage, sound, artist stipends, not abstract "festival support").

3. Prepare real-time leaderboard screenshots in batch
   - Have graphics template ready showing Artizen project rank + vote count + match pool % filled.
   - Plan to update and post at hours 12, 24, 36, 48.

4. Set up Discord + Telegram pinned message structure
   - Single pinned Artizen link + emoji-reaction guide.
   - All questions to threaded reply under pinned post only (prevents message chaos).

### Launch Day, Hour 0 (10:00 AM ET)
1. Post Twitter/Farcaster thread (4 parts over 30 minutes)
   - Part 1 (10:00): Hook with data or mission statement ("188 ZAO members funding free festivals for independent artists. Artizen match unlocks 3x impact per $1. Here's how.")
   - Part 2 (10:10): Personal story (Zaal on why this festival matters to you).
   - Part 3 (10:20): Voting mechanics explained (screenshot showing $10 = 100 votes + match cascade).
   - Part 4 (10:30): Direct call to action with Artizen link + mention @ArtizenFund.

2. Send Telegram/Discord staggered announcement wave
   - T+0: Discord thread in #announcements channel with artifact visual + link.
   - T+10 min: Telegram main group with match mechanics framing + link (different angle than Discord).
   - T+20 min: Farcaster channel (if separate) with short quote-boosted format.

3. Coordinate Super Core member buys
   - Monitor in real time. If fewer than 3-5 buys in first 30 minutes, DM reluctant anchors individually (no shame).
   - Goal: $100-300 raised in first hour = visible momentum signal.

### Hours 1-12 (10:00 AM - 10:00 PM ET)
1. Post real-time leaderboard update (hour 12)
   - Screenshot current Artifact sales count + $ raised + fund rank.
   - Farcaster/Telegram caption: "LIVE UPDATE: We're at $X, now top [rank] in [Fund Name]. 36 hours left. Momentum is real."

2. Release creator story video (60 seconds)
   - Zaal on phone, vertical format, speaking directly: why ZAOstock matters + what the match funds unlock + Oct 3 details.
   - Auto-caption via YouTube or Descript.
   - Post natively to Twitter (video embed, max engagement) + Discord embed + Telegram loop (2-3x).

3. Begin 12-hour cadence updates
   - 12:00 PM (2h): Video launch + artist testimonial post.
   - 4:00 PM (6h): Match fund transparency post ("Here's why $50k in Greenpill Fund matters to us").
   - 8:00 PM (10h): Community highlight UGC request ("Share why YOU believe in ZAOstock in comments, tag a friend").

### Hours 12-24
1. Hour 24 milestone update
   - Screenshot leaderboard + vote count.
   - Farcaster/Telegram: "24h update: Hit $X, now top 5. Momentum locked. Last 24h sprint: help us push to top 15%."

2. Deploy first co-promotion (if partner projects confirmed)
   - Coordinate boost with 1-2 aligned Artizen projects in same fund.
   - Shared graphic: "Supporting [Partner] on Artizen. Music builders deserve funding. [Mutual link]"
   - Post to both projects' audiences (staggered 12 hours apart to reach both communities).

3. Begin daily update cadence
   - Post once daily (9:00 AM or 5:00 PM ET) with: current $ raised, leaderboard position, one new angle (artist spotlight, match-fund explainer, behind-the-scenes clip, community highlight, stretch-goal unlock).

### Hours 24-48
1. Hour 36 momentum post
   - Farcaster/Telegram: "$X raised, top 30% locked. Last 12 hours to push to top 15%. Supporters who bought become ZABAL Games advisors + get early artist lineup access."
   - Shift language from "buy" to "join the movement."

2. Hour 48 final push (last 12 hours)
   - Increase posting to 2-3x daily.
   - 36h post: "Final 12 hours. We're at $X. Match funds close at midnight Thursday."
   - 42h post: "6 hours left. Momentum is real. Push to $Y to lock this in."
   - 48h post: "CLOSED. Final tally: $[total], finished rank [#]. Thank you [tag anchors + key supporters]."

## 2. GET SHARED: Multi-Channel Amplification + Personal Outreach

### Staggered Multi-Channel Announcement Wave (12-Hour Rollout)
Structure this to reach each audience where they pay attention, not as spam.

**Hour 0 - Twitter/Farcaster (10:00 AM ET)**
- 4-part thread (see Launch Plan, hours 0-0.5).
- Mandatory: tag @ArtizenFund in opening tweet.
- Include Artifact visual (square GIF/video, no text overlay).

**Hour 3 - Discord (1:00 PM ET)**
- Channel: #announcements or #fundraising.
- Tone: "ZAO Festivals LIVE on Artizen. We're competing in We're Loud DIY Festivals + other funds ($50K match total). First 48 hours critical. Please retweet/share if you vibe. [Link]"
- Pin message for duration of campaign.

**Hour 6 - Telegram (4:00 PM ET)**
- Main group message + pinned.
- Angle: progress + match math. "We hit $X in 3 hours. Here's leaderboard standing [screenshot]. Can you share with friends?"

**Hour 10 - Email (if list exists, 8:00 PM ET)**
- Subject: "ZAO Festivals on Artizen: 38 hours left."
- Body: mission + match mechanics + link + one social proof (current $ raised, vote count, leaderboard position).

**Rationale**: Four touchpoints over 10 hours instead of one dump = 2-3x conversions per audience size. Each channel gets messaging tailored to user mindset (Twitter = newsy, Discord = community consensus, Telegram = progress/FOMO, email = trust).

### Tiered Personal Outreach (Segmented Community)

**Super Core (10-15 people, personal DM, 24h BEFORE launch)**
- Farcaster/Telegram DM to each person individually.
- Reference something specific they've done or said recently.
- Example: "Hey [Name], I saw your post on independent artists last week—that's exactly the energy ZAOstock needs. We launch tomorrow on Artizen, and if you can grab an artifact in the first few hours, it signals momentum to everyone else. No pressure. [Link, sent tomorrow at 10am]"
- Goal: emotional commitment, not transactional.

**Core (30-40 people, group Telegram, 2h BEFORE)**
- Tag @Core crew or similar.
- Message: "Tomorrow 10am ET, ZAO Festivals goes live on Artizen. This is real. Competing for $50k in match funding. If you believe in the festival, grab an artifact. First 48h = everything. [Link dropped at 10am]"
- Tone: factual urgency, not emotional manipulation.

**Warm (remaining members, public Telegram/Discord, 1h BEFORE)**
- Public channel, no special framing needed.
- "ZAO Festivals is LIVE on Artizen. This is ZAOstock funding. First 48h window. If you're down, here's how: [Link]"

**Expected outcome**: Super Core commits early (signals momentum), Core follows (social proof), Warm joins (herding behavior). This sequencing increases likelihood of first-hour $100-300 anchor buy significantly.

## 3. GET ACCEPTED + CURATED: Voting Mobilization + Fund Director Strategy

### Multi-Fund Submission Blitz Strategy

**Do NOT submit to one fund and wait for results.** Submit to ALL eligible funds in parallel (or staggered by 1-2 weeks for fresher momentum windows).

**Identify eligible funds** (verify current artizen.fund/matchfunds):
1. We're Loud DIY Festivals ($50K match, primary fit)
2. Greenpill Gatherings (regen, community focus)
3. Funding the Commons (cultural infrastructure)
4. Global Music (artist support, international angle if applicable)
5. Apollo (innovation, cultural tech)
6. ZAO Fund for Emerging Culture (your own fund, automatic but submit formally)
7. Collective Women's (if applicable, women-led organizing)

**Each fund curates independently.** Submit ZAOstock to 5-7 funds = 5-7 separate vote pools to tap, stacking match multipliers.

**Worst case**: curated by 1-2 funds, unlocking 2x match ($10 sale + $10 fund match).
**Best case**: curated by 5-7 funds, unlocking $50-70 match per $10 artifact (industry high).

### Fund-Specific Narrative Customization

Do NOT use identical submissions. Spend 20-30 minutes per fund to reframe ZAOstock to match each fund's curation rubric.

**Greenpill Fund (Regenerative Gatherings)**
- Lead with: "ZAO Festivals is a regenerative gathering that heals culture through music and community ownership."
- Emphasize: on-chain coordination model (transparent payouts to artists), local rootedness (Ellsworth, Maine), community healing as the output.
- Tone: deep ecology, local resilience, cultural commons.

**Hubs Network (Independent Physical Spaces)**

**Funding the Commons (Cultural Infrastructure)**

- Emphasize: public-good framing (free entry), commons model (participatory curation), tech-as-enabler (Artizen match funds).

**Apollo (Innovation + Cultural Tech)**

## 4. ENRICHMENT: Backer Rewards, Pitch Deck, Positioning, Sponsor Framing

**Tier 1: $10 Artifact (Base)**
- Name listed on ZAO Festivals "Supporters" page with linked Farcaster handle (zero-cost recognition).

**Tier 2: $50 Artifact or Higher**
- Name + 20-word bio snippet featured in ZAO social shoutout thread.

- Festival day: "Collector's Lounge" access with branded beverage, merch variant, 5-min artist meet-and-greet rotation.

- Release Artifacts in themed batches on fixed schedule: Week 1 "Lineup Reveal" (Mon-Wed only), Week 2 "Venue Snapshot" (Fri-Sun only), Week 3 "Artist Collab" (final 72 hours).

- One 1-hour video call with Zaal during June-July 2026 (5 slots maximum) to discuss festival goals, ZAO vision, or collaboration ideas.

## 5. THE SINGLE MOST IMPORTANT MOVES (Top 5)

Rank these by impact. Execute in order.

### Move 1: Multi-Fund Submission Blitz (Apply to ALL Eligible Funds Simultaneously)

**Why it's #1**: Artizen's curation (per docs 844, 847) is fund-specific. Each fund curates top 30% independently. Applying to 1 fund = 1 vote pool. Applying to 5-7 funds = 5-7 vote pools + stacked match multipliers.

- Example math: $10 artifact sale.
  - Submitted to 1 fund (We're Loud DIY only): $10 + $10 match = $20 total.
  - Submitted to 5 funds all curated: $10 + $50 match ($10 per fund) = $60 total.

**Action**:
- Identify all eligible funds (We're Loud DIY, Greenpill, Funding Commons, Global Music, Apollo, ZAO Fund, Collective Women's).
- Write custom 100-300 word narrative for EACH fund (not identical submissions).
- Use ONE artifact (video/GIF) with fund-specific captions.
- Submit to 1-2 funds in Week 1, 1-2 in Week 2, remainder in Week 3.
- Stagger so each submission gets 2-week focused voting push (not all at once).

**Expected outcome**: Curated by 3-4 funds minimum. Match multipliers stack. ZAOstock hits $15K-25K target with reasonable community effort.

**Why it's #2**: Boost Score algorithm (per doc 844) likely weights early velocity. First 60 minutes determine visibility across all 5+ fund ecosystems.

**Expected outcome**: $150-300 in first 60 minutes + visible "top tier" leaderboard position for days 1-3 = triggers FOMO in Tier 2/3 community members = 2-3x multiplier on organic buys.

### Move 3: Community Voting Orchestration (Manual Vote Blitz During Curation Phase)

**Why it's #3**: Voting power drives curation tier. Artizen explicitly states "voting" as the curation signal, not sales (sales come after curation gate passes).

**Expected outcome**: 2,000-3,000 votes in opening week (easily achievable) = top-30% curation tier locked = project advances to competition phase where match funds activate.
