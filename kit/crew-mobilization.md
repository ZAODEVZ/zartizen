# Crew mobilization - the GC-ready rally

The single most important lever on Artizen is the crowd. The prize goes to the project that sells the MOST
Artifacts (count of buyers), and signups + contributions earn the boosts that drive visibility. Many people
each putting in $10 beats a few big checks. This file is the copy you drop in the group chat to move people.

## The 3 steps (paste this in the GC)

<paste>
Help me back our artists on Artizen - takes 3 minutes:

1. SIGN UP at artizen.thezao.com (just signing up gives you voting power that helps our projects rank).
2. BUY the $10 Artifact of the project we are pushing this week: [PROJECT LINK]. $10, that is it.
3. BOOST + reshare it - hit boost on the project and repost so your friends see it.

Why it matters: every $10 you spend gets DOUBLED by our fund (and stacks with other funds). The project that
sells the most wins a cash prize. We win by showing up together. Let's go.
</paste>

## How to run a rally (for whoever is leading)

- **Pick ONE project per drive.** Do not split the crowd. Name the horse, link it, repeat it.
- **Time it to a high-multiplier drive** (2x / 3x weeks) so each $10 deploys more match. Check the live drive +
  deadline first.
- **Make the ask tiny and concrete.** "$10 + boost on THIS link" beats "go support our stuff."
- **Lead from the front.** The leader buys + boosts first, screenshots it, posts the proof.
- **Close the loop.** After the rally, post the result: "we moved X buyers, the project climbed to #Y, $Z match
  unlocked." Proof builds momentum for the next one.
