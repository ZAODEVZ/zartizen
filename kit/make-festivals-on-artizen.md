## PART B - Create the FUND: ZAO Festivals Fund

This is an application, not a self-serve toggle. Path: apply to the "$1M for Community Funds" program
-> call with René -> up to $50K seed on approval. Full proposal: kit/fund-proposal.md. Call agenda +
the 3 questions to settle: kit/rene-call-brief.md.

The fund settings to enter when approved:
- **Name:** ZAO Festivals Fund
- **Mission:** Back the artists and organizers who gather people in the real world - music festivals,
  concerts, pop-up shows, residencies, and cultural activations - and help them fund it without losing
  ownership. Artist-first, IRL, community-powered.
- **Eligibility:** real-world gathering/activation; creator-owned; community participation; build in
  public; fair artist compensation; a public-facing culmination.
- **Seed:** $50K Artizen + ZAO treasury as a Founding Sponsor (confirm on the call).

The /festivals page (zaoartizen.vercel.app/festivals) is already live to recruit organizers + sponsors
into it before launch.
