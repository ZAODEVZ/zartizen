## B. Video Disruptor grant proposal (stack this - up to $15k, paid in LPT/Arbitrum)

Submit per the grant process (proposal -> committee review 2-4 weeks -> 20% upfront + milestones). Terms to
accept: open-source, "Powered by Livepeer" watermark on the stream, ~3-month timeline, LPT payout on Arbitrum.

**Project:** ZAOstock Live - an open-source, decentralized festival livestream on Livepeer.

**What we are building:** a reusable, open-source streaming pipeline that broadcasts a full-day live music
festival over Livepeer - multi-set, all-day, "Powered by Livepeer" watermark - and is documented so any
community festival can reuse it. ZAOstock (Oct 3, Ellsworth Maine, free) is the live proving ground.

**Why it fits "novel web3 video application" + real demand:** most Livepeer demos are synthetic. This is a real
free festival with real viewers (1K+ target), all-day load, in a high-traffic tourist region - genuine
demand on the network, plus an open-source toolkit other events inherit.

**Milestones (~3 months to Oct 3):**
1. Stand up the Livepeer streaming pipeline + a test broadcast (watermark live).
2. Dry-run stream (a smaller event / soundcheck) under real conditions.
3. Live festival broadcast - Oct 3, full day, on Livepeer.
4. Open-source the toolkit + ship a recap; document for reuse.

**Deliverables:** the open-source repo, the live Oct 3 broadcast, the watermarked stream, a recap reel, and a
"how to livestream your festival on Livepeer" writeup.

**Team:** ZAOstock livestream circle (Mickey / Thy Revolution - livestream lead; Iman - livestream/ops).

**Amount:** up to $15,000 (Video Disruptor), LPT on Arbitrum.
