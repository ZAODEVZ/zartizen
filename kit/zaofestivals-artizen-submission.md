## Logline (short)

## Description / story
ZAO Festivals brings independent artists to real stages. We're a series of free, artist-built music festivals
run by The ZAO. We did ZAO-PALOOZA in New York (during NFT NYC, 12 artists, the community's first IRL meetup)
and ZAO-CHELLA in Miami (during Art Basel, in Wynwood - WaveWarZ LIVE and AR art). This October our flagship
lands in Maine: ZAOstock 2026 - free, all-day, downtown Ellsworth, part of Art of Ellsworth weekend, at the
gateway to Acadia.

We treat a festival as a protocol, not a product: free to attend, artists paid fairly and transparently, and the
crowd that funds it owns it. Back this and you help bring independent artists to the stage - a fair-paid slot,
travel and production support, and a recording, photos, and a recap reel each artist takes home.

This is the next wave of community-owned culture, built in public. See the whole story: zaostock.com/festivals

## How this collaborates with The ZAO (the ZAO Fund requirement)
ZAO Festivals IS a ZAO project - produced by The ZAO, built by a 27-person ZAO team and the ZAO bloc, with the
lineup drawn from ZAO-community artists (WaveWarZ, COC Concertz, and more). It is collaboration with The ZAO by
definition.

## How it uses emerging tech
- A livestream of the whole festival (target 1K+ viewers; exploring decentralized streaming via Livepeer).
- On-chain attendance collectibles / Magnets for the people who show up.
- WaveWarZ music battles as a live, on-chain element.
- Build-in-public: the team, lineup, and standings are all public (zaostock.com).

## Real-world activation
ZAOstock 2026 - Saturday October 3, 2026, downtown Ellsworth, Maine. Free, outdoor, all-day, family-friendly,
part of Art of Ellsworth weekend. Targets 200-400 in person + 1K+ livestream.
